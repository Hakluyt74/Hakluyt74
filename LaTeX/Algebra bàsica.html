Funcions trigonomètriques bàsiques

Per un angle $\theta$ en un triangle rectangle:

\sin \theta &= \frac{\text{catet oposat}}{\text{hipotenusa}} \
\cos \theta &= \frac{\text{catet adjacent}}{\text{hipotenusa}} \ \tan \theta &= \frac{\sin \theta}{\cos \theta} = \frac{\text{catet oposat}}{\text{catet adjacent}} \end{align*} Funcions rec`{i}proques: \begin{align*} \csc \theta &= \frac{1}{\sin \theta} \ \sec \theta &= \frac{1}{\cos \theta} \ \cot \theta &= \frac{1}{\tan \theta} = \frac{\cos \theta}{\sin \theta} \end{align*} \section{Identitats fonamentals} \begin{align*} \sin^2 \theta + \cos^2 \theta &= 1 \ 1 + \tan^2 \theta &= \sec^2 \theta \ 1 + \cot^2 \theta &= \csc^2 \theta \end{align*} \section{F'ormules de suma i difer`{e}ncia d’angles} \begin{align*} \sin(A + B) &= \sin A \cos B + \cos A \sin B \ \cos(A + B) &= \cos A \cos B - \sin A \sin B \ \tan(A + B) &= \frac{\tan A + \tan B}{1 - \tan A \tan B} \ \sin(A - B) &= \sin A \cos B - \cos A \sin B \ \cos(A - B) &= \cos A \cos B + \sin A \sin B \ \tan(A - B) &= \frac{\tan A - \tan B}{1 + \tan A \tan B} \end{align*} \section{F'ormules del doble d’un angle} \begin{align*} \sin(2A) &= 2 \sin A \cos A \ \cos(2A) &= \cos^2 A - \sin^2 A = 2\cos^2 A - 1 = 1 - 2\sin^2 A \ \tan(2A) &= \frac{2\tan A}{1 - \tan^2 A} \end{align*} \section{F'ormules del mig angle} \begin{align*} \sin\left(\frac{A}{2}\right) &= \pm\sqrt{\frac{1 - \cos A}{2}} \ \cos\left(\frac{A}{2}\right) &= \pm\sqrt{\frac{1 + \cos A}{2}} \ \tan\left(\frac{A}{2}\right) &= \pm\sqrt{\frac{1 - \cos A}{1 + \cos A}} \end{align*} \section{Llei dels sinus i cosinus} \begin{align*} \frac{a}{\sin A} &= \frac{b}{\sin B} = \frac{c}{\sin C} \quad \text{(Llei dels sinus)} \ c^2 &= a^2 + b^2 - 2ab \cos C \quad \text{(Llei del cosinus)} \end{align*} \section{Transformacions de productes en sumes i difer`{e}ncies} \begin{align*} \sin A \sin B &= \frac{1}{2} [\cos (A - B) - \cos (A + B)] \ \cos A \cos B &= \frac{1}{2} [\cos (A - B) + \cos (A + B)] \ \sin A \cos B &= \frac{1}{2} [\sin (A + B) + \sin (A - B)] \end{align*}
